## Bug Found & Fixed
Default value for --dns option was "system, mdns, http" (single string with spaces)
causing "invalid value" parse error when starting proxy with no arguments.
Fixed: changed `default_value = "system, mdns, http"` to `default_values = ["system", "mdns", "http"]`
in genmeta-proxy/src/lib.rs line 24.
